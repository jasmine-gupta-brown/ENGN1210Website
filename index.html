Course Overview

ENGN 1210 is designed to introduce students to the fundamental principles of engineering, including problem-solving, design, and communication skills. This course will lay the foundation for future study in various engineering disciplines.

Course Topics Include:

  • Engineering problem-solving techniques
  • Basic design principles
  • Ethics in engineering
  • Teamwork and collaboration
