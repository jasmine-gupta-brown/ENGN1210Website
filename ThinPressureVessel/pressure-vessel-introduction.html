Blood vessels, airways, ureters are examples of pressurized cylindrical tubes in biology.
Many bio-medical/clinical devices such as

  • Heart/lung ventilator tubes

  • Kidney dialysis units

  • Catheters for balloon angioplasty

  • IV pumps utilize pressurized tubes

Stresses from internal pressures are important to predict/quantify!
